## Academic Profile

Mehjabin Shirin is a doctoral researcher in Optometry and Vision Sciences at the University of Hyderabad. Her work focuses on visual perceptual, psychophysical, and retinal biomarkers associated with early Alzheimer's disease and dementia in the Indian population. Her research connects laboratory-based vision science with clinical and community-oriented questions in cognitive ageing, neuro-ophthalmology, and non-invasive biomarker development.

Her methodological interests include colour perception, face perception, visual psychophysics, eye tracking, pupillometry, electrodermal activity, heart-rate variability, OCT-based retinal assessment, and experimental task design using Python, MATLAB, and PsychoPy.
